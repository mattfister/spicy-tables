About Spicy Tables

Spicy Tables is a collection of random roll tables designed to spice up role playing games. It was built by Matt Fister. It is built on Tracery, by Kate Compton, {{ mustache }}, bootsrap, and jquery. The source is available at Github. It is easy to fork and build your own generators! Contact me for info.
